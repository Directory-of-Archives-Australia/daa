Contact information

All queries about a particular archives collection should be directed to the specific archives group concerned.

Not all groups have an email contact, so you may need to make contact via telephone, fax or regular mail. These details, where provided, are clearly listed in their Directory entry.

Corrections and additions to entries in the Directory of Archives in Australia should be sent to office@archivists.org.au
